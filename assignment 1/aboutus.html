About JG Saree Heaven

JG Saree Heaven is a leading online retailer of sarees and traditional Indian clothing. We offer a wide range of high-quality sarees, salwar kameez, lehengas, and other traditional Indian outfits for women of all ages. Our mission is to provide our customers with the latest fashion trends, the best quality products, and exceptional customer service.

Our History

JG Saree Heaven was founded in 2010 by JG Begum, a fashion designer and entrepreneur with a passion for traditional Indian clothing. Jahan saw a need for an online platform that would make it easy for women around the world to find and purchase high-quality sarees and other traditional Indian outfits. With a small team of designers and developers, Jahan launched Jahan's Saree Heaven in 2010, and the company has been growing ever since.

Our Team

JG Saree Heaven is proud to have a team of talented and dedicated professionals who are passionate about fashion and customer service. Our team includes designers, developers, customer service representatives, and logistics experts who work together to ensure that our customers have a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.

Our Values

At JG Saree Heaven, we are committed to the following values:

  • Quality: We strive to offer only the highest-quality sarees and traditional Indian outfits, using only the finest materials and craftsmanship.
  • Customer Service: We are dedicated to providing exceptional customer service, and we are always available to answer any questions or concerns our customers may have.
  • Innovation: We are constantly seeking new and innovative ways to improve our products, services, and customer experience.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to reducing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of our business.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading online retailer of sarees and traditional Indian clothing, providing our customers with the latest fashion trends, the best quality products, and exceptional customer service. We strive to be a trusted and respected brand in the Indian fashion industry, and to make a positive impact on the lives of our customers, employees, and the communities we serve.
